How to purchase a token on the exchange.

Instructions to purchase a token on the exchange:

Pancakeswap:

  1. Follow the link: https://pancakeswap.finance/swap
  2. On the site, bind your wallet Metamask or any other you use.
  3. At the top, click on the "drop arrow" and select the coin for which you will purchase the AOUAD token.
  4. In the bottom line, click on "drop arrow" and in the search string enter the contract address "0xfdcefe633b641191ebe71511268c79d776a2fee6", in the token string "AOUAD" press "Import", in the window appears and tick "I understand" and click "Import".
  5. At the top line you will see the amount you plan to purchase the token for.
  6. Click "Swap". Confirm the operation in your wallet.
  7. To see the AOUAD token in your wallet, you need to press "Import token" inside the wallet and enter the contract address "0xfdcefe633b641191ebe71511268c79d776a2fee6" and confirm the addition of the token.
  8. Done. You have purchased the AOUAD token.

Purchase instructions via the website:

To track the transaction, in the form of MY Wallets specify the public address of the wallet from which the transfer will be made.

Make a transfer of cryptocurrency to the wallet specified in the form (AOUAD) addresses.

After sending funds and fully confirming your online trust, within 24 hours tokens will be credited to your wallet balance.

Be careful when transferring funds. Always check the network, currency and wallet from and to which you transfer.

Cryptocurrency transactions are irreversible and if you send the money to the wrong address — we can not help you in any way.
